==================================== Prometheus Flame ====================================

Prometheus Flame is a content expansion plugin that introduces the Southern Periphery, a new region south of the Rim which became vital to the Free Worlds war effort and later the development of human civilization. New storylines, a New Ships, Outfits and much more are planned for this plugin.

This is the third attempt for a Plugin that I had envisioned before life decided to pull me in other directions and now that I now have more free time and more experience I decided to try again.
Prometheus Flame is a plugin that aims to overhaul the main campaign and human content in general to make it relevant for mid and late game while introducing new ships, factions, storylines and lore fleshing out the world of Endless Sky.
